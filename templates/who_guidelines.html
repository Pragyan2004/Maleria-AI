WHO Guidelines

Global Malaria Screening Protocols 2026

Core Recommendation Framework

1. Universal Parasite Screening

WHO recommends that all suspected malaria cases should be confirmed by a parasite diagnostic test (microscopy or RDT) before treatment.

2. Artemisinin-based Combination Therapy (ACT)

Uncomplicated P. falciparum malaria should be treated with an artemisinin-based combination therapy (ACT).

3. Vector Control Measures

Emphasis on sleeping under insecticide-treated nets (ITNs) and indoor residual spraying (IRS).
