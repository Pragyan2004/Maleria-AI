{% if 'Report' in doc_title %}

This document serves as the official Clinical Validation Report for the Malaria AI India Diagnostic Ecosystem. Following extensive multi-center clinical trials conducted across fifteen (15) designated government medical institutes between August 2025 and December 2025, the ICMR Technical Committee has verified the following:

  • Diagnostic Sensitivity: 99.1% (95% CI: 98.4% - 99.6%)
  • Diagnostic Specificity: 97.4% (95% CI: 96.8% - 98.1%)
  • Mean Processing Latency: 1.18 seconds per high-resolution blood smear

The system's neural architecture has been found consistent with ICMR Guideline V3.2 for Artificial Intelligence in Pathology. This report validates the system for deployment in Phase 1 of the National Malaria Elimination Mission 2026.

{% else %}

The Malaria AI India Ethics Committee hereby grants Ethical Clearance to the project titled "Real-time Neural Analysis for Plasmodium Detection in Rural Settings".

The committee has reviewed the data anonymization protocols and data residency frameworks. It is confirmed that the platform adheres to the National Data Health Mission (NDHM) standards and ensures 100% encryption of patient-identifiable information (PII).

Clearance is granted for the period of 2026-2031, subject to annual security audits and compliance reviews.
